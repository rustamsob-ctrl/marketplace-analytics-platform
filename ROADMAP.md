# Development Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Project Foundation

- Define architecture
- Prepare database schema
- Configure development environment
- Add authentication
- Add organizations and user roles

## Phase 2 — Product and Inventory Management

- Product catalog
- Marketplace product identifiers
- Warehouse stock
- Marketplace stock synchronization
- Minimum stock settings
- Stock history
- Low-stock notifications

## Phase 3 — Sales Analytics

- Orders and sales
- Revenue
- Marketplace commissions
- Logistics expenses
- Cost of goods
- Estimated profit
- Product-level analytics
- Daily and monthly reports

## Phase 4 — Forecasting and Shipment Planning

- Average daily sales
- Days of stock remaining
- Recommended shipment quantity
- Warehouse and regional grouping
- Shipment priority
- Out-of-stock monitoring

## Phase 5 — Supplier Portal

- Supplier accounts
- Purchase requests
- Order statuses
- Product availability
- Shipment coordination
- Documents and comments

## Phase 6 — Pricing Tools

- Marketplace price monitoring
- Price history
- Pricing rules
- Repricing recommendations
- Minimum margin protection

## Phase 7 — Public Release

- Installation documentation
- Contributor guide
- Demo environment
- Automated tests
- Issue templates
- First stable open-source release
